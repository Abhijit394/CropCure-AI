About CropCure AI

Empowering farmers with AI-driven crop health insights.

Our Mission

At CropCure AI, our goal is to revolutionize agriculture through technology. We believe every farmer deserves access to intelligent, data-driven insights to maximize crop yield and sustainability.

How CropCure AI Works

1. Capture

Farmers upload leaf photos of their crops using our platform.

2. Analyze

Our AI model detects diseases and nutrient deficiencies instantly.

3. Recommend

Receive treatment advice and preventive care tips in seconds.
